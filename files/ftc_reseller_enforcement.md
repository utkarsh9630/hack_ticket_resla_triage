# FTC Ticket Reseller Enforcement — Guidance Source

**Source:** Federal Trade Commission  
**URL:** https://www.ftc.gov/  
**Topic:** Deceptive practices by ticket resellers

---

## Overview

The FTC has taken enforcement action against ticket resellers and platforms that engage in deceptive pricing, fake urgency, and misrepresentation of official status. Under the FTC Act, unfair or deceptive acts in commerce are prohibited.

---

## Key Enforcement Areas

### 1. Drip Pricing
Resellers may advertise a low base price and add large fees at checkout. The FTC requires that the full price — including all fees — be disclosed upfront. If a seller gives you a price and then adds significant charges at payment, that may be deceptive.

### 2. Fake Official Affiliation
Some resellers use names and branding that implies they are the official box office or venue-authorized seller when they are not. This is deceptive.

**What to look for:**
- URL does not match the venue or official artist website
- Seller claims to be "authorized" but cannot provide verification
- Branding mimics official ticket outlets

### 3. False Scarcity
Fake countdown timers, false "only X left" messages, and fabricated competing buyer pressure are deceptive tactics that may violate FTC rules.

### 4. Cancellation and Refund Misrepresentation
Resellers must clearly disclose their refund and cancellation policies. "All sales final" buried in fine print after a purchase may be challengeable.

---

## Consumer Rights and Remedies

- **Credit card chargebacks:** If you paid by credit card and did not receive what was promised, contact your card issuer to dispute the charge. This is one of the strongest consumer protections available.
- **FTC complaint:** File at **reportfraud.ftc.gov**
- **State AG:** Many state attorneys general have consumer protection divisions that handle ticket fraud

---

## Key Risk Signals (Reseller Fraud)

| Signal | Risk Level |
|---|---|
| Seller claims to be official but redirects off-platform | HIGH |
| Price significantly higher than face value with hidden fees | MEDIUM |
| Fake urgency ("only 2 left", countdown timer) | MEDIUM |
| No clear refund policy stated | MEDIUM |
| Credit card not accepted — only irreversible methods | HIGH |
